About the Project

TariPredict is an analytical platform built with Django and machine learning models trained on the global Stack Overflow Developer Survey. Its objective is to provide insights into the technology job market and offer salary predictions based on professional experience, education, country, and other relevant factors.

The project combines data science techniques with a modern web interface to support professionals in making informed career decisions.
